05.02.01 Foodsharing Copenhagen

This was definitely the most fun project to make!
We did a collaboration with Food Sharing Copenhagen:
we had to pick a topic, either try to get more people to be volunteers or spread awareness about food waste.
We build a website around that topic and filmed a video that could advertise our idea.
A good group and great teamwork have never been more important.
I have always been into filming and editing video but I never really had the chance to do it so this assignment was perfect for me.
I took full responsibility for the video production and editing.
Filming the video with the Food Sharing Copenhagen volunteers themselves was the key! My whole group was really happy with the video.

05.02.03 Storyboard

Before making the video we had to make a storyboard.
I've never made a storyboard for a video before.
It definitely helped me a lot, having a storyline to follow when you're "on set" filming is very nice and can save you lots of time.
On the other hand, I learned that sometimes things don't go as planned on the storyboard so you more than often will have to improvise.

05.02.04 Customer pitch

Now, the website is ready, the video is ready, everything's ready!
Time to give a pitch presentation to Food Sharing Copenhagen itself!
We decided to divide the presentation into two parts: first with PDFs slides and then show the actual website.
We kept the slides really simple. We didn't want to have a lot of text on them, we rather speak and tell the customers what our idea is about than having them reading our slides.
The best part of the Customer Pitch was that at the end everybody immediately gave us feedback on our presentation and project.
Now we know what to fix for the next time!
